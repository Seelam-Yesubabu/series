BAKI HANMA

Baki Hanma a young, powerful martial artist who strives to surpass his legendary, and incredibly strong, father Yujiro Hanma. He trains relentlessly, using various fighting styles, and faces tough opponents, including escaped convicts and even prehistoric warriors. Baki's goal is not just to be strong, but to defeat his father in combat.
